About DEdC


DEdC is a team originated concept for CPE 495/496 working to create the next generation DFD modeling software. Though in its infancy, our team's goal is to bring DFD modeling into the 21st Century through a combination of deep understanding in team collaboration and extensibility techniques.


Why DFD editing?


DFD creation is a major component of the current software or system design paradigm. If you are an I.T. professional deploying a network, or a development team creating new software, or an engineer designing a new mission-critical system, it is imperative that you understand the layout of your system before it comes under attack. DFD planning will play a crucial first step in this process.


Our Software


The DEdC tool is still in the early stages of development. We will soon be releasing a very early alpha with basic DFD editing functionality, including a team based, collaborative DFD editor.
